4. Why BeanBliss Collects Personal Information and How It Uses It

BeanBliss collects and uses personal information as necessary for the purposes for which it was obtained. BeanBliss may use your personal information for some or all of the following purposes:

  • Orders – to process and ship your orders and inform you about the status of the same. Please note that there are e-commerce websites offering BeanBliss products but are not controlled or operated by BeanBliss. We recommend that you read their policies, including those on privacy, before making purchases on such sites.
  • Account maintenance – to create and maintain your accounts with us, including the management of consumer loyalty or reward programs associated with your account.
  • Customer service – to provide you with customer service, including responses to your inquiries, complaints, and general feedback about our products. Customer service may be provided through various communication methods, including email, letter, phone, and online chat.
  • Consumer engagement – to allow you to be more actively engaged with our products and services. This may include using or posting consumer-generated content.
  • Personalization – BeanBliss may combine personal information about you from one source (e.g., a website) with information obtained from another source (e.g., an offline event). This provides BeanBliss with a more complete view of you as a consumer, which, in turn, allows BeanBliss to serve you better and more personally, including in relation to the following points:
    • Websites – to improve and personalize your experience on websites, using, for example, data such as account login information, technical information about your computer, and/or previous website usage information;
    • Products – to improve BeanBliss products, tailor them to your needs, and generate new product ideas. This includes using demographic information, consumer profile information, and consumer feedback; and
    • Interest-based advertising – to serve you ads tailored to your interests. One way BeanBliss does this is by matching activities or information collected on BeanBliss Sites with data collected about you on third-party sites (i.e., matching browsers or devices believed to be related to each other). This type of advertising is also called "online behavioral advertising" or "targeted advertising." Such personalization is typically carried out by using cookies or similar technologies.
  • Marketing communications – to provide you with marketing communications where you have opted-in. These can include information about BeanBliss products and services, competitions, and promotions. Such communications may be shared via electronic means (e.g., SMS, email, online ads, etc.) and regular mail.
  • Social features – to provide you with several social features, including:
    • Website community features – when you visit BeanBliss Sites and use community features such as forums and blogs, you may share personal information with other community members and interact with them;
    • Third-party social networking – BeanBliss may collect your personal information from a third-party social network when you use the social network’s features integrated with BeanBliss Sites, or if you participate in one of our social networking features (such as our Facebook page). You can learn more about how these features work, and the profile data BeanBliss may obtain about you, by visiting the website of the relevant third-party social network.
  • Other specific purposes – We may use your personal information for other specific business purposes, including maintaining the day-to-day operation and security of BeanBliss Sites, conducting demographic studies or audits, and contacting you for consumer research.
